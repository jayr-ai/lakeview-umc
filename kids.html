Lakeview Kids

Big truths, made for little hearts

During Sunday worship, your kids are cared for by trained, background-checked volunteers. Each week they sing, play, hear a Bible story at their level, and make a friend or two. You worship with peace of mind; they leave asking when they can come back.

What to Expect

Lakeview Kids at a glance

👤

Age-appropriate classes

Separate rooms and lessons for nursery, preschool, and elementary so every child learns at their level.

🔒

Safe & secure

Every volunteer is vetted, and our check-in and check-out keeps your child matched only to you.

🎗

Fun that sticks

Games, songs, and crafts that make the Bible come alive, so faith feels like joy, not a chore.
