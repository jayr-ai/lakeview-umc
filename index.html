A United Methodist Congregation

Come as you are. There's a seat by the water for you.

Lakeview United Methodist Church is a warm, growing family in Lipa City, Batangas where honest questions, open hearts, and everyday people meet the grace of God. Whether you have been in church your whole life or have never set foot in one, you belong here.

Welcome

A church built on grace, not perfection

We are ordinary people learning to follow Jesus together. As Methodists, we believe grace goes before us, meets us, and grows us. You will find real friendship, thoughtful teaching, and a community that shows up for one another Monday through Saturday too.

"My hope is that the moment you walk in, you feel less like a visitor and more like you're coming home."
- Rev. Jesus Villamin, Pastor
